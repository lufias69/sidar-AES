================================================================================
INTERPRETATION GUIDELINES
================================================================================

ICC (Intraclass Correlation Coefficient):
  ICC(2,1) - Single measure consistency
  ICC(2,k) - Average of k measures consistency
  < 0.50: Poor reliability
  0.50-0.75: Moderate reliability
  0.75-0.90: Good reliability
  > 0.90: Excellent reliability

Cronbach's Alpha:
  Measures internal consistency
  < 0.60: Unacceptable
  0.60-0.70: Questionable
  0.70-0.80: Acceptable
  0.80-0.90: Good
  > 0.90: Excellent

Fleiss' Kappa:
  Multi-rater agreement (10 trials as raters)
  < 0.00: Poor agreement
  0.00-0.20: Slight agreement
  0.21-0.40: Fair agreement
  0.41-0.60: Moderate agreement
  0.61-0.80: Substantial agreement
  0.81-1.00: Almost perfect agreement

Variance Decomposition:
  Between-Trial: Variance due to differences between trials
  Within-Trial: Variance within each trial (student/question differences)
  Lower between-trial % = Higher consistency across trials
