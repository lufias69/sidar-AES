OVERALL RECOMMENDATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

PRIMARY RECOMMENDATION: GEMINI-1.5-PRO

Advantages:
  1. Superior accuracy: 80.4% vs 69.1% exact agreement
  2. Better consistency: ICC = 0.993 vs 0.989
  3. Lower error rate: 19.6% vs 30.9%

Use Cases:
  • Small to medium classes (30-100 students)
  • Large institutional deployments (1000+ students)
  • High-stakes assessments requiring accuracy
  • Budget-conscious implementations

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

ALTERNATIVE: CHATGPT-4O

Consider if:
  • Already integrated with ChatGPT ecosystem
  • Require specific ChatGPT features
  • 69.1% accuracy acceptable for use case

================================================================================
IMPLEMENTATION CONSIDERATIONS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Technical Requirements:
  • API access to chosen model (ChatGPT or Gemini)
  • Secure storage for student essays and rubrics
  • Error handling and retry logic
  • Rate limiting compliance
  • Result validation and human review workflow

Best Practices:
  1. Use lenient strategy (proven superior consistency)
  2. Implement human oversight for borderline cases
  3. Maintain audit logs of all gradings
  4. Regular calibration with expert graders
  5. Transparent communication with students

Quality Assurance:
  • Random sampling for expert review (10-20%)
  • Flag disagreements > 1 grade for review
  • Monitor consistency across batches
  • Periodic revalidation studies
