## üí™ MOTIVATION & TIPS

### Stay on Track
- Set daily goals (small, achievable)
- Use Pomodoro technique (25 min work, 5 min break)
- Write in focused sessions
- Don't aim for perfection in first draft
- Celebrate small wins

### Writing Tips
- Start with the easiest section
- Use the passive voice sparingly
- One idea per sentence
- One topic per paragraph
- Use concrete examples
- Show, don't just tell

### Common Pitfalls to Avoid
- Over-claiming results
- Under-citing prior work
- Ignoring limitations
- Inconsistent terminology
- Missing details in methods
- Figures without clear purpose

### Quality Markers for IEEE TLT
- Clear research contribution
- Rigorous methodology
- Statistical validity
- Practical applicability
- Ethical considerations
- Reproducible research
